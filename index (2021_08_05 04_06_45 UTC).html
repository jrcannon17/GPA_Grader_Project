How It Works

For any user, for the current semester, type in the number of hours under the semester hoursfield. The letter grades for that semester will be calculated into points (see side bar for letter grade conversion). Add up the points of all classes and put the sum in the pointsfield. This will give you your current GPA for that semester. If you want us to add it up for you, click ADD IT UP! and follow the prompt.

Once completed, you will have an option of saving the score. If you choose to do so, then you will be redirected to create an account and log in, where your score will be appended. This will allow you to keep track of your GPA as you complete each semester. It is from there that you will get a cumulative GPA that you can keep track of.
